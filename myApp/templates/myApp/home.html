How Aira works

Record your visit

Press record and leave it running while you talk with your doctor. No note-taking. No trying to remember every detail.

Get a clear explanation

Aira turns the conversation into a simple, easy-to-understand summary in your preferred language.

Leave with your next steps

You'll get important follow-up questions, reminders, and topics worth discussing at your next appointment.

Built with privacy in mind

Recording should only happen when everyone in the room agrees. Aira is designed to help you remember your visit — audio is not stored longer than necessary to create your summary.

Grounded in trusted medical knowledge

Every explanation is checked against trusted medical sources, including Mayo Clinic, Johns Hopkins Medicine, and Cleveland Clinic, helping make complex information easier to understand.

A memory tool — not medical advice

Aira helps you remember what was discussed and prepare for follow-up conversations. Always follow the guidance of your healthcare team.

For patients, families, and caregivers

Whether you're the patient, helping a loved one, or attending as a caregiver, Aira helps everyone leave with the same understanding.
